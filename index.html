National Achievement Survey (NAS) is a nationally representative large-scale survey of students' learning undertaken by the Ministry of Education, Government of India. NAS gives a system level reflection on effectiveness of school education. Findings help compare the performance across spectrum and across population in order to find the desirable direction for improvements. National Achievement Survey-2021 is scheduled on 12th November 2021. The National Council of Educational Research and Training (NCERT) is involved in the development of Assessment Framework for gauging the competencies attained by the children vis-a-vis learning outcomes. This national level survey would be conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE) as Assessment Administrator for Grade III, V, VIII and X students of govt., govt-aided and private unaided recognized schools. The Survey will be conducted in a monitored environment in the sampled schools. Selection of schools is based on UDISE+2019-20 data. States, Districts used for drawing of the samples for NAS 2021 are therefore, as per the UDISE+2019-20.
